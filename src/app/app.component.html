Lightweight

This library is lightweight and easy to use. Add the directive ngxAccordion as a wrapper, and ngxAccordionLabel and ngxAccordionContent in the template.

Single or multiple accordions, you chose! Just duplicate the label and content directives inside the same ngxAccordion if you opt for a multiple accordion.

Service

Get a full control on your accordion! A service exposes a toggle method to open partially or totally an accordion, close or open a particular tab in an accordion, or close or open all tabs in an accordion.

The service also exposes RxJs Subjects that let you notified over the status of a particular tab (open or close) or all tabs in an accordion.
